# **Questions from them**

- Tell we about yourself?
I am Yichia Hsieh. I got my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in NTUST and I majored in EE. After graduation, I got my first job in Mediatek as a Wifi software engineer. My responsibility is to support our customers to solve their problems with our WiFi chipsets, so I have to deal with any kind of wifi related issues. Like I supported Ubiquiti's 7915 project, this is the first IAP project of 7915, or I supported Cameo/Elecom as EasyMesh alphasite. Besides customer support, I also developed features, such as I designed 7915 PREK and VLAN Group Key. After 3.5 years working at Mediatek, I joined Plume in this March. My job is to join our customers' development and integrate OpenSync into their platform. One of our customers use 7615 and 7915 and adopt open source mt76 driver for 3 projects, so I implemented the target layer for mt76 driver and integrate OpenSync to their platform and get the OpenSync certification. So that's pretty much about myself.

Because I long for life in the States, and I want to explore more. I know this will be a big challenge for me, I have to leave behind my current life and start a whole new life in the States, but even so I still want to do it because I think I am an adaptive person and I can overcome the challenges. I was an exchange student in Czech Republic for 6 months back in college, and at that time I broaden my horizon and explore different cultures. I traveled to many different countries and make a lot of friends. It was a very special experience and I've learned a lot. This time it will be an opportunity in a lifetime to contribute what I've learned in the past 4 years, and also to fulfill my dream to work in the States.
