### Term

Nsts = Number of Space Time Streams

Nss = Number of Spatial Streams

STBC = Space Time Block Coding


### Nsts > Nss?

- **When STBC active, Nsts > Nss**

When NSS < NSTS, the space time block coder (STBC) distributes the spatial streams to
the space time streams using a diversity coding scheme.
In the spatial mapping block, the space time streams are divided among the TX
antennas. The standard provides three modes for this purpose:

- Direct mapping (1-to-1 mapping from space time stream to TX)
- Spatial expansion (multiplication with a matrix)
- Beamforming
