FEM，Front-end Modules，即就是前端模块。硬件电路中的前端模块完成射频信号的发送放大以及接收放大（with bypass）、滤波，甚至包含功率检测、控制和开关的这样一个作用。对于Wi-Fi产品，FEM分为2.4G FEM和5G FEM，分别应用于2.4G和5G频段。


[https://www.microsemi.com/product-directory/rf-microwave-a-millimeter-wave/1284-wlan-front-end-modules](https://www.microsemi.com/product-directory/rf-microwave-a-millimeter-wave/1284-wlan-front-end-modules)

WLAN (Wi-Fi) front-end modules integrate multiple devices required to implement an RF front end. Typically included are one or more power amplifiers with both input/output impedance matching, a switch, and one or more low-noise amplifiers.
